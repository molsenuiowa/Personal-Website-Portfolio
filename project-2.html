2023 Precious Metals Analysis: Gold & Silver

Code Link

This chart demonstrates the significant fluctuations in trading volumes, with gold typically showing higher peaks compared to silver, reflecting gold's dominant market presence.

The line chart reveals the daily fluctuations in gold and silver prices, with both metals showing a notable correlation of 65%, indicating synchronized market movements.

The density plot illustrates the distribution of daily price changes, where both commodities exhibit a central tendency around zero, but with gold generally having a denser and sharper peak, suggesting less volatility compared to silver.

Project Overview

This data-driven analysis explores the correlation, trading volumes, and price volatility of gold and silver using statistical techniques and visualizations in R. By examining daily price changes, monthly trading volumes, and candlestick patterns, the project provides insights into market trends and behaviors, aiding stakeholders in making informed investment decisions.
